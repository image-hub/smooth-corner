How to Use Smooth Corner

  1. Upload your image by dragging & dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the corner radius using the slider
  3. Choose a background color (for JPEG format)
  4. Select your preferred output format
  5. Click "Download Image" to save your rounded image
